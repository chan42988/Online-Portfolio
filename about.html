As a result, I ended up double majoring in Communication and English. I also pledged for Alpha Kappa Psi Professional Business Fraternity, and experienced a nine-week process of honing professional business skills (e.g., interviewing, public speaking, resume building). I became the Marketing Director/Webmaster for a year.

During my internship I was offered a chance to interview for a contract position at Facebook Austin. I passed the interview process, and worked at Facebook Austin under a year contract as an Ads Integrity Analyst. After my contract was done I decided to move back to the east coast and eventually found another contract position as a Digital Analyst at HS Ad (formerly known as LG Ad).

As of right now, I finished my contract position at HS Ad, and am looking to switch careers from Digital Advertising to Website/App/Web App development/design.
